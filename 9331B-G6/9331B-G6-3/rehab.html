REHABILITATION

WEEKLY REPORT

The drug personnel that has been caught by the officials of the BCPO and barangay are directed
to the barangay office to report weekly so that they can monitor each drug personnels. They
are subjected to community service in which they will help in clean-up drives, community
outreach for the people who are in need and more. They are also required to help the officials
with the rescue and medication of the victims whenever calamities strike. They are required
to report weekly by showing up on the appointment set by the head of the barangay. The sole
purpose of this program is to track the drug personnels and at the same time, they can help
the community with their own little ways. It is a way of the barangay to divert the drug personnels
from doing drugs again.

COUNSELING

Once the drug personnel has been caught, he/she is required to attend the counseling by the
local priest that is set by the barangay head. There session will last from up to a week. Each
session will consists of bible reading, bible sharing, sharing of life experiences that will be
preached by each drug personnels and the local priest itself. The sole purpose of this program
is to strengthen their faith and bring back their relationship with God. It also helps them
avoid temptations through the bible teachings.

DRUG TESTING

The drug personnels that has been caught must take a drug test once in a while
so that the barangay head will know whether the drug personnel is still associated
with drug related cases or not. This will help the officials if the usage of drugs
in the barangay is still rampant. Once the drug personnel has been tested positive,
he will undertake procedures with accordance to the law itself.

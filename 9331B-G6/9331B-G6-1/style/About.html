City Camp Proper is one of the 129 Barangay within Baguio City,Benguet. It is situated near City Camp Central, Palma-Urbano, Lower Rock Quarry, Lower QM and Legarda Road. As of 2018, the Barangay was headed by the Honorable Aurora G. Abellera and seven barangay kagawad namely Jaime S. Burtarde, Teresita B. Ellamil, Yolanda E. Ellamil, Isabelito G. Angara, Lolita V. Ariola, Virginia B. Ferrer and Elvira N. Ocampo. In addition, Ricardo S. Angara acts as their Barangay Secretary, Carla Faye G. Fernandez as their Barangay Treasurer, Monica N. Sicat as their BNO/BNS and Nora B. Bangkilas as their Barangay Chief Tanod.

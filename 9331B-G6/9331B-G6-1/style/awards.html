City Camp Proper

Awards

The City Camp Proper, headed by Hon. Aurora G. Abellera has been recognized for being competent, excelent and one of the friendliest barangay in Baguio city. Back in 2011, in celebration of Women's Month, the City Government of Baguio conducted a search on outstanding women leaders of Baguio City held on March 8, 2011. One of th awardees was Hon. Aurora G. Abellera she was awarded the Public Service in recognition for the efforts in her field that contributed to the development of their barangay. A plethora of awards doesn't only prove that barangay City Camp Proper is one of the outstanding barangay in Baguio City. In return for all their efforts and their dedication in fulfilling their tasks in serving the community they have been nominated and won numerous awards.
